Synopsis

Join New Yorks very own Philip J. Fry and friend's in their sometimes life threatening journeys across the universe. Fry, a young pizza delivery guy finds himself 1000 years into the future after accidentally freezing himself within a secret cryogenic freezer. After waking up he seeks out his only living relative Professor Farnsworth who happens to be his very distant grand nephew. Professor Farnsworth hires him to join his intergalactic delivery company Planet Express where he meets the rest of the gang. Join Fry as he tries to find his placement in this new world!
